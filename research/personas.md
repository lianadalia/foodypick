# User Personas — foodypick Helsinki
## Complete Empathy Maps

**Project:** foodypick
**Date:** 2026-03-03
**Market:** Helsinki metropolitan area — city centre and suburbs (Espoo, Vantaa)
**Research basis:** Finnish food delivery market data, Statistics Finland consumer surveys, Wolt/Foodora App Store reviews, Finnish food culture research, HOK-Elanto retail studies

---

## Finnish Context Notes

These personas are grounded in specific characteristics of the Helsinki food delivery market:

- **Dominant platforms:** Wolt (market leader), Foodora (merged into Wolt 2022); K-Ruoka and S-kauppa for grocery delivery
- **Finnish meal rhythm:** Lunch (lounas) 10:30–12:00, dinner (illallinen/päivällinen) 16:30–18:30 — earlier than most European countries
- **Seasonal extremes:** Kaamos (polar night, Nov–Jan) drives indoor eating; kesä (summer) brings terassi culture and outdoor grilling — delivery demand shifts dramatically
- **Food traditions:** Hernekeitto on Thursdays, lohikeitto year-round, karjalanpiirakka as snack culture, Kotipizza as domestic comfort food, Hesburger as local fast food
- **Consumer habits:** High trust threshold before trying new services, loyalty to tried-and-tested restaurants, strong value-for-money expectations (Finns are price-transparent shoppers)
- **Infrastructure:** Metro (länsimetro to Espoo), trams, buses — delivery radius and ETA vary significantly city centre vs. suburbs

### Empathy Map

#### 💬 Says

- *"I've been vegan for two years — I need the dietary filters to actually work, not just show me salads."*
- *"Wolt has the best selection in Punavuori but honestly their service fee has gone up twice this year."*
- *"I always check if a restaurant has proper vegan options, not just 'we can remove the cheese'."*
- *"I ordered at 18:30 and the app said 25 minutes — it arrived at 19:20. That's not acceptable."*

#### 🧠 Thinks

- *"If this place doesn't label allergens properly I'm not risking it — I've had bad experiences."*
- *"Why does a vegan bowl cost €3 more on delivery than on the restaurant's own website?"*
- *"I wonder if this new ramen place is consistent or if it's just good photos."*
- *"It's dark and raining, I'm not going out. I need this to arrive before 19:00 so I can eat before the Netflix thing starts."*
- *"I should try something new this week instead of just ordering from Sandro's again."*
- *"If I order now during kaamos it'll be dark out anyway so I might as well just stay in."*

#### ❤️ Feels

- **Curious** when browsing new restaurants — discovery is enjoyable when the UI supports it
- **Frustrated** when vegan options are buried or mislabelled — she's been served non-vegan food twice
- **Loyal and warm** toward the 4–5 restaurants she trusts completely
- **Impatient** when ETAs slip past 40 minutes without explanation on a weeknight
- **Guilty** occasionally about the packaging waste, even when restaurants use eco-packaging
- **Cosy and content** in the depths of Finnish winter when a hot bowl arrives at her door

#### 👁️ Does

- Opens Wolt around 17:45–18:15 on weekdays, after the busiest project delivery windows
- Filters by "vegan" first, then sorts by ETA, then checks photos of the actual dish
- Screenshots menus she wants to try later and shares them in her design team Slack
- Reads item descriptions word by word to check for hidden animal ingredients
- Always rates her orders — gives 5 stars generously and writes comments when something is wrong
- Meal-preps on Sundays in autumn and winter to reduce ordering frequency; abandons this by November
- In summer, prefers to pick up from the restaurant herself — the walk to Hietalahdentori is pleasant and she saves the delivery fee

#### 🚧 Pains

- **Dietary filter accuracy:** Restaurants label items "vegan" when they contain honey, stock, or shared fryers — she's been caught out
- **Price inflation:** Same bowl €13.50 in restaurant, €16.80 on delivery after fees — feels dishonest
- **Inconsistent ETAs:** Particularly bad on Tuesday and Thursday evenings; she suspects too few riders in Punavuori
- **Packaging waste:** eco-packaging claims are unverifiable; she uses this to rationalise ordering less frequently but still orders
- **Limited late-night options:** After 22:00 the good plant-based restaurants close, leaving only kebab and pizza
- **Dark patterns:** "Add a dessert?" modal on checkout every single time

#### 🌟 Gains

- A reliable, searchable list of confirmed plant-based restaurants with accurate labelling
- Price transparency shown before she adds anything to cart (delivery fee + service fee upfront)
- Consistent ETAs she can plan an evening around
- Seasonal menus that reflect Finnish produce (sienisaato in autumn, fresh pea dishes in summer)
- The ability to rate individual menu items, not just the whole order
- A "my usual" shortcut that surfaces her top 3 restaurants without browsing

- Traditional Finnish restaurant category — lohikeitto, hernekeitto, karjalanpiirakka, silakka
- Single-portion options clearly labelled
- A visible phone number for the restaurant and for support
- Email receipt to her actual email address without needing to configure anything
- Saved payment and address that works every time without re-entry

- A trust and allergen-compliance score for restaurants based on community reports
- Single dashboard for dual orders — one checkout, clear separation of delivery addresses if needed
- Seasonal Finnish food curation: mushroom season in autumn, fresh fish in summer, comfort soups in winter
